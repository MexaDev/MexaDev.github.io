Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

Kücki's Pub is ideally suited for functions and special events. Our facilities can cater for up to 180 people. We have 2 floors, an outside deck and offer a full restaurant service. For those who enjoy the party vibe, there are two bars to choose from. Our two big screen TV’s make any football or rugby match a live event, especially if coupled with a good meal and fresh draught. We have a wine bar for reservations for those that want a more quiet, private and smarter venue. However, should you wish to have it a little more quiet, we also cater for a relaxed family dinner with your loved one’s and friends. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we will gladly be of assistance.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our great staff and atmosphere the moment you walk inside until you finish your last beer or dessert. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some great vibes.
